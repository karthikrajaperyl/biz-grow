# **App Name**: BizGrow Digital

## Core Features:

- Agency Information Display: Present all static content, including services, industries served, 'how we help' process, portfolio/case studies, pricing packages, and client testimonials, with clear CTAs.
- AI-Driven Business Audit Tool: A generative AI tool to provide instant, personalized preliminary digital audit reports for local businesses based on user inputs.
- Online Contact & Consultation Scheduling: Allow prospective clients to easily submit inquiries and book free consultations through dedicated forms and calendar integrations.
- Integrated WhatsApp Communication: Provide a direct WhatsApp chat option on the website for quick, real-time communication with the agency.
- Dynamic Blog for Insights: A section dedicated to publishing articles on marketing tips and local business success stories to engage visitors and demonstrate expertise.
- SEO-Optimized & Mobile-First Design: Implement core SEO best practices for better search engine visibility and ensure a fully responsive, mobile-first user experience across all devices.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant, clear blue (#4085F6) for calls-to-action and key interactive elements, evoking a modern tech aesthetic and reliability. Chosen to stand out against a dark background.
- Background color: A very dark gray-blue (#21252A), offering a sophisticated and professional backdrop that complements the modern tech palette and enhances content readability in a dark scheme.
- Accent color: A deep, rich purple (#3E0CBB) to add depth and a dynamic, forward-thinking feel. Used sparingly for highlights or gradient effects, it harmonizes with the primary blue while providing strong visual contrast.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), for bold, tech-inspired headlines that command attention and convey a modern, innovative image. Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), for its clean, objective, and highly readable qualities, ensuring clarity across all informational content.
- Utilize sleek, modern line-art icons for services and industries, maintaining a professional yet friendly aesthetic that aligns with the digital agency's branding.
- Implement a clean, grid-based layout with ample white space for a contemporary feel. Prioritize mobile-first responsiveness to ensure optimal viewing and interaction on all device sizes, with bold headlines and clear call-to-action buttons for high engagement.
- Incorporate subtle hover effects on interactive elements such as service cards, CTAs, and navigation links. Employ smooth, professional transitions for scrolling and section changes to enhance user experience without being distracting.
